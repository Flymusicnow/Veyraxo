# Veyraxo brand

## Core idea
Veyraxo is an AI execution system that turns ideas into commercial traction through offer validation, sprint planning, asset generation, outreach workflows, and revenue-focused accountability.

## Meaning
Veyraxo = direction + action + outcome.

## Tagline
Turn ideas into traction.

## Source of truth
SVG is the brand source of truth during early product development.

Use:
- `assets/brand/veyraxo-logo-master.svg` as the master logo file.
- `public/brand/veyraxo-logo.svg` for browser/app display.

Do not add new PNG or ICO files until the identity is locked.
